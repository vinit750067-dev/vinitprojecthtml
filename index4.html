★HTML (nested list and tables)

  • HTML mein nested lists aur tables ka use karke aap complex data ko structured tarah se present kar sakte hain.

* Nested Lists:

  • Nested lists mein ek list ke andar doosri list hoti hai. Yeh unordered lists (ul) aur ordered lists (ol) dono ke saath kaam karti hai.
Example:
  • Item 1
    • Subitem 1
    • Subitem 2
  • Item 2
    1. Subitem 1
    2. Subitem 2

* Tables:

  • Tables mein data ko rows aur columns mein organize kiya jata hai.
Examlple:
Name Age
John 25
Jane 30

* Nested Tables:

  • Nested tables mein ek table ke andar doosri table hoti hai.
Example:
Name Age Details
John 25
Address Phone
New York 123-456-7890
